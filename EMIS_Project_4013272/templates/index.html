LSBU Exam Management Information System
This is the information page and contains:
  1. Rules for the students to know.
  2. Procedures to follow in exam conditions.
For Students: These rules and exam procedures are generalised, yet will prepare your mind for what to do when you arrive.
For Invigilators: Log in to see the specific exam procedures.
Exam Rules
  1. No communication across to fellow students.
  2. All phones and devices must be turned off and left in the bag.
  3. No eating during the exam.
  4. Ask an invigilator before going to the toilet.
  5. Whisper when communicating with invigilators.
General Exam Procedures
  1. A summary of the rules for examination candidates are displayed in every examination room.
  2. Examination papers are prepared within the timetable set out in the assessment calendar approved by the Academic Board.
  3. The academic member of staff responsible for the examination paper is responsible for checking and certifying the accuracy of the final version of the paper.
  4. No unauthorised member of staff can copy any papers before the day of the examination.
  5. If a student causes a disturbance, the invigilator will:
    • Warn the student.
    • Require the student to withdraw (from exam hall) is he or she persists
    • Not re-admit, if the student is required to withdraw.
    • Fill in the online exam report form, to note the circumstances of the exam, for the university
